Common Questions

Go to the Forgot Password page, enter your email address, and click "Send Reset Link." You'll receive an email within 2 minutes with a secure link to create a new password. The link expires after 1 hour.

In your dashboard, navigate to People → Members → Invite. You can invite individuals by email or do a bulk import using a CSV file. Invited users will receive an email with a secure onboarding link that expires after 7 days.

Go to Settings → Integrations → Google Workspace, then click "Connect." You'll be redirected to Google's OAuth screen to authorize lvlBase. You need to be a Google Workspace admin to complete this integration.

Go to Settings → Data Management → Export. You can export all data as CSV or JSON. Enterprise customers can also set up automated data exports to S3, GCS, or Azure Blob Storage on a schedule.

lvlBase runs entirely in the browser — no installation required. We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the mobile app, we require iOS 14+ or Android 9+. Minimum bandwidth recommendation: 5 Mbps for optimal performance.
